Visit Abidjan

Learn more about Ivoirian culture in one of the biggest cities in West Africa

Top three activities to do in Abidjan

Visit Cathédrale Saint-Paul

The biggest Cathedral in West Africa with gorgeous mural decorations and architecture inside.

Explore Banco National Park

Discover many species of tropical plants as well as fauna in what Ivorians call the "Green Lung" of the city.

Get Ivorian artifacts and souvenirs

Visit the Artisanal Center of the City of Abidjan to get some Ivoirian mementos such as statues, masks or clothing.
